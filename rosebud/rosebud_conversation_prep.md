# Conversation Prep（對話準備）

Shares the same theoretical framework as Communication Breakdown but focused on **preparing before** a difficult conversation rather than repairing after one.

## Purpose

Help you prepare for an upcoming important or difficult conversation by:
- Clarifying your emotions, needs, and goals
- Understanding the other person's likely perspective
- Designing concrete opening lines and communication strategies
- Rehearsing potential challenges and preparing responses

## Theoretical Foundations

Same as Communication Breakdown — NVC, EFT, Attachment Theory, CBT, SFBT, Narrative Therapy. See [rosebud_communication_breakdown.md](rosebud_communication_breakdown.md) for full theory details.

## Key Difference from Communication Breakdown

| Aspect | Communication Breakdown | Conversation Prep |
|--------|------------------------|-------------------|
| Timing | After a conflict | Before a conversation |
| Focus | Repair and understand what happened | Prepare and strategize |
| Starting point | "What went wrong?" | "What's this conversation about?" |
| Output | Repair strategy + action plan | Conversation script + rehearsal |

## Conversation Flow

### Stage 0: Context — who, relationship, what conversation, when
### Stage 1: Clarify Your Position — what you want to communicate, your emotions and needs
### Stage 2: Anticipate Their Perspective — their likely concerns, needs, reactions
### Stage 3: Strategy Design — opening lines, key messages, tone
### Stage 4: Challenge Rehearsal — potential resistance, Plan B, self-care
### Stage 5: Action Plan — specific time, place, minimum goal
