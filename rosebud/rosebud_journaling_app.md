# Rosebud - AI Journaling & Personal Growth App

## Overview
An AI-powered journaling and personal growth app that uses conversational AI to guide users through journaling sessions, analyze entries, identify emotional patterns, and provide mentorship-style insights.

## Company Details
- **Founded:** 2023
- **Co-Founders:**
  - Chrys Bader (Y Combinator alum, co-founder of Secret)
  - Sean Dadashi (UC Berkeley Cognitive Science graduate)
- **Website:** https://www.rosebud.app/

## Funding
- $6M seed round (2025) led by Bessemer Venture Partners
- Participating investors: 776, Initialized Capital, Fuel Capital, Avenir, Tim Ferriss

## Key Features
- **Chat-Based Journaling:** AI-guided conversational journaling sessions
- **Pattern Analysis:** AI analysis of journal entries to identify emotional patterns and triggers
- **Smart Mood Tracker:** Understand emotional patterns over time
- **Intention Setting:** Set intentions with reminders and check-ins
- **Voice Journaling:** Available in 20+ languages
- **Weekly Reports:** Progress reports summarizing insights
- **Premium Features:**
  - Long-term memory
  - Voice and call modes

## AI Journaling Capabilities
See [rosebud_journal_ai_capabilities.md](rosebud_journal_ai_capabilities.md) for full details.

### Journal Types & Conversation Modes
- **Morning Intention** - prepare for the day
- **Evening Reflection** - review and debrief
- **Identifying Needs** - explore psychological needs
- **Free Conversation** - record thoughts anytime

### Core Analysis
- **Pattern Recognition:** emotion fluctuations, behavior patterns, triggers, time management habits
- **Cognitive Distortion Scanning:** all-or-nothing thinking, overgeneralization, catastrophizing, "should" tyranny, emotional reasoning, labeling
- **Goal Decomposition & Tracking:** break goals into 5-15 min steps, If-Then strategies, cross-entry progress tracking

### Interaction & Guidance
- **Questioning Strategies:** noise-reducing, leverage, reflective, pragmatic
- **Structured Response Format:** patterns observed → smallest lever action (≤20 min) → 2 If-Then strategies → one closing line
- **Language:** voice-to-text (Traditional Chinese), understands mixed Chinese/English, responds in Traditional Chinese, tone is casual/empathetic/direct/pragmatic

### Memory & Continuity
- Remembers: personal profile, last 5 journal summaries, selected historical excerpts, special requests
- Cannot remember: details outside current conversation, unwritten thoughts, other Rosebud app settings

### Special Features
- Time management reminders (limit planning/browsing to 30 min–1 hr)
- Reading integration (summarize books, apply to 8-week plan)
- Research support (data analysis, IRB, questionnaires)
- Flexible recording (no forced fields, encourages completeness)
- Cross-day linking (morning summarizes previous evening, uses recent journals for continuity)
