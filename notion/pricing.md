# Notion - Pricing (2026)

| Plan | Price | Key Features |
|------|-------|-------------|
| **Free** | $0 | Unlimited pages/blocks, 5MB file upload limit, 7-day page history, up to 10 guests |
| **Plus** | $10/user/month (annual) / $12 (monthly) | Unlimited file uploads, 30-day version history, unlimited guests |
| **Business** | $20/user/month (annual) / $24 (monthly) | Unlimited Notion AI included, unlimited version history, advanced collaboration, SAML SSO |
| **Enterprise** | Custom pricing | Advanced security, analytics, user provisioning (SCIM), audit log, dedicated support |

## Important Notes
- **May 2025 change:** Notion AI moved from a separate add-on to being included exclusively in Business and Enterprise plans
- Free and Plus users no longer have access to AI features
- Custom Agents (Notion 3.3) are free on Business/Enterprise through May 3, 2026; will use Notion credits after
