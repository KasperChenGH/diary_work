# Notion - Company Details

## History
- **Founded:** 2013 by Ivan Zhao, Chris Prucha, Jessica Lam, Simon Last, and Toby Schachman
- **First Public Release:** 2016
- **Headquarters:** San Francisco, California
- **CEO:** Ivan Zhao (retains ~30% ownership — unusually high for a venture-backed founder)
- **Employees:** ~5,643 (as of February 2026)

## Key Metrics
- 100M+ users worldwide
- 4M+ paying customers
- $400M annual revenue (2024)
- Over 50% of Fortune 500 companies have teams using Notion
