# Notion - Research Overview

## What is Notion?
Notion is an all-in-one productivity and collaboration platform that combines wikis, documents, databases, project management, calendars, email, and AI-powered agents into a single connected workspace. It serves individuals, teams, and enterprises with 100M+ users worldwide.
