# Notion - Core Features

## Wikis
- Categorize and organize pages with properties, tags, and verification status
- Default properties: Owner, Last edited date, Tags, and Verification
- Self-serve knowledge base for SOPs, onboarding guides, brand guidelines, etc.

## Docs
- Rich text editing with embedded media, code blocks, and nested pages
- Real-time collaboration and commenting
- Templates for common document types

## Databases
- Multiple views: tables, Kanban boards, timelines, calendars, galleries, lists, and map view
- Relational database system linking tables together (e.g., CRM with contacts, companies, deals)
- Custom properties, filters, sorts, formulas, and rollups
- Automations triggered by database changes

## Projects & Task Management
- Kanban boards, timelines (Gantt-style), and sprint planning
- Task assignment, due dates, priorities, and status tracking
- Database-powered project tracking with cross-linking

## Forms
- Conditional logic support
- Responses feed directly into Notion databases

## Notion Calendar
- Two-way sync with Google Calendar and Outlook
- Integrated with workspace content and Notion Mail
- Schedule command for meeting coordination

## Notion Mail (launched April 2025)
- Gmail-based email client with Notion AI integration
- Auto-organizes inbox, drafts replies, schedules meetings
- Save emails directly to Notion pages
