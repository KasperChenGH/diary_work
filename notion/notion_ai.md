# Notion AI

## AI Agents (Notion 3.0, September 2025)
- Autonomous agents that work for up to 20 minutes on multi-step tasks
- Can operate across hundreds of pages simultaneously
- Build project plans, compile feedback from multiple sources, create interconnected page structures
- Evolved from "AI that suggests" to "AI that executes"

## Custom Agents (Notion 3.3, February 2026)
- Users can create custom agents with access to specific sources (Notion, Slack, Mail, Calendar, MCP integrations)
- Agents answer questions and take actions autonomously
- Free on Business/Enterprise plans through May 3, 2026; will use Notion credits after

## Content & Writing Assistance
- Generate text from scratch, rewrite for clarity/tone/length
- Summarize notes and documents
- Translate and check text
- Adjust tone and style

## Image Generation & Editing
- Generate cover images, photos, charts, diagrams, and slide visuals
- Refine with follow-up prompts

## AI Meeting Notes
- Automatic transcription and summaries
- Action item extraction

## Enterprise Search
- AI-powered search across pages, messages, files, and connected apps

## Mobile AI (Notion 3.2, January 2026)
- Full AI agent capabilities on mobile
- One-tap transcription, summaries, action items

## AI Models
- GPT-5.4 available in model picker (OpenAI's latest as of early 2026)
- Multiple model options for different use cases

## Availability
- AI features are only available on **Business** and **Enterprise** plans (as of May 2025)
- Free and Plus users do not have access to AI features
