# Diary Log

Plain-text log of the user's journal conversations. Rolling log of the last 150 entries. Each entry records the date/time, template, persona, and the user's own words (what they shared during the session — not Claude's responses).

This is the user's personal diary archive. Claude appends to this after every journal session.

**Rules:**
- Keep only the **last 150 entries** (delete oldest when adding new)
- Record the user's words, not Claude's questions or responses
- Include date and time for every entry
- Keep each entry concise but complete — capture what the user actually said/felt/experienced
