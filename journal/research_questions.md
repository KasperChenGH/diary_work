# Research Questions Tracker

Persistent log of open research questions. Claude reads this at the start of each academic session to prioritize searches, connect findings to existing questions, and track progress over time.

**Rules:**
- Add new questions when they emerge during academic or journal sessions
- Update status as papers are found and answers develop
- Link related papers by Zotero key or title
- Archive answered questions (move to bottom) rather than deleting
